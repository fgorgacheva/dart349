The midterm proposal consists of three parts: the proposal essay including research on the topic, the style guide, and the UX-UI design. This project will be about story telling and testing my skills in making something I've never attempted before. Using JS and other sub-libraries, I want to recreate and retell a story that my mother used to tell me when I was young. Frankly, many people missed out on this story, and I think more should learn about it.

In the essay portion, I've researched about why we, humans, like stories so much and what makes a compelling story in order to explain my choice, and quite honestly, explain to myself why stories compell me so much.

The style guide compiles my sources of inspiration as well as the artistic vision and direction I plan on taking.

Finally, the UX-UI design contains samples of my main ideas for the UI of my story application as well as some sketches of potential animations I will be attempting to include.
